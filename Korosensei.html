The Overview

Koro-Sensei is the enigmatic and eccentric teacher of Classroom 3-E in the series Assassination Classroom, a popular manga and anime. With his bright yellow, octopus-like appearance, he immediately stands out, but his personality is even more surprising—he's cheerful, compassionate, and incredibly skilled as a teacher. However, there's a twist: he has threatened to destroy the Earth unless his students can assassinate him by the end of the school year. Despite his ultimatum, Koro-Sensei's true nature and motivations are shrouded in mystery, making him both a mentor and a target for his students. This page serves as a character overview and fan exploration of his abilities, personality, and role in the story. For a deeper dive into his fascinating backstory, you’ll have to watch the show or read the manga—it’s worth it!
