How You Can Help

If you're reading this, thank you for taking the time to even consider helping out Fan Arc. Truly, I appreciate it.

Now to get down to it, as of right now there are a couple ways to contribute, depending on what you would like to do.

  • Write and Share Content: Have a deep dive on a character, a show a fan theory, or fan fiction? Great! Write it out in your own words, short or lengthy, detalied or surface level its all up to you!
  • Submit Artwork: Are you an artist? Do you want your work front and center for people to see? Do you want them to know you made it? Well so do I and I will do my utmost to ensure your work gets the credit it deserves!
  • Improve the Code: If you're into coding, Well, then you probably know more then I do and i'd love to have some help! whether you know one lanuage, one line or a million of them, Fan Arc would Love your help!
  • Share Ideas: Have an idea for a feature or improvement? Let us know! We're always looking for ways to make the site better.
