How to Submit Your Work

You can send your content in the following ways:

Email Submission

Send your work to Official.devanb13@gmail.com. Make sure to include any details you'd like in the credit such as socials, name, etc.

  1. Prepare your content in a common format (DOC, PDF, JPG, PNG)
  2. Include a title and brief description
  3. Specify which section of the site it belongs to
  4. Add your preferred attribution details
  5. Send to our email with "FanArcs Submission" in the subject line

GitHub Contribution

If you are familiar with GitHub, you can fork our repository, make changes, and submit a pull request.

  1. Fork the FanArcs repository
  2. Create a new branch for your changes
  3. Make your additions or improvements
  4. Submit a pull request with a clear description
  5. Wait for review and feedback

Submission Form

If you'd prefer a more structured submission process, fill out our content submission form.

  1. Go to our submission form (coming soon)
  2. Fill out the required fields
  3. Upload any necessary files
  4. Submit the form
  5. Receive a confirmation email

What Happens Next?

Once we receive your submission, we'll review it and get back to you as soon as possible. If everything checks out, we'll add it to the site and credit you accordingly.

Everyone who contributes will get special recognition‚Äîwhether it's through a tag, a mention in the content itself, or any other requests you may have. We want to make sure your hard work is noticed!

1. Review

We'll review your submission for quality, relevance, and appropriateness.

2. Feedback

We may provide feedback or suggestions for improvements if needed.

3. Publication

Once approved, we'll publish your content with proper attribution.

4. Promotion

We'll help promote your contribution across our platform.
