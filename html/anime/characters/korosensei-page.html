Backstory

Real Name: Ryushi Korogane. His Upbringing is that of a rough, mysterious and even cruel. He was born unaware of who he was entirely and, nobody knows the name of where he grew, up. Why? well i'll leave that for you to decide.

Didn't know anything about himself as a child. He didn't know his name, birthday, or even his family. As far as I'm aware, we don't know why, and all we can do is speculate. However, we do know what this environment caused within his ideologies... Blood. Money. Talent. The things that had once made him feel lost and confused, had now shown him the light to get out.
